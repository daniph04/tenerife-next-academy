### Why plan ahead
- Compliance: NCAA permits one international trip every four years.  
- Timing: Best windows are after spring semester or just before fall preseason.  
- ROI: Use the tour to combine high-performance training, quality matches, and cultural time that bonds your roster.

### Why Tenerife
- Climate: Year-round training weather, no extreme heat or cold.  
- Facilities: FIFA-standard pitches, Olympic pool, performance gym, recovery zones.  
- Connectivity: Direct European links and safe, welcoming environment.
